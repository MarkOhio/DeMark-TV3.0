How to Play Memory Card Game

Game Rules:

  • Flip two cards at a time to find matching pairs
  • When you find a match, the cards stay flipped
  • Try to find all pairs with the fewest moves
  • Use the Hint button (costs 10 points) to briefly see all cards

Difficulty Levels:

  • Easy: 4×4 grid (8 pairs) - 7 second preview
  • Medium: 6×6 grid (18 pairs) - 5 second preview
  • Hard: 8×8 grid (32 pairs) - 3 second preview

Tips:

  • Remember the positions of cards as you flip them
  • Plan your moves to minimize flipping the same cards multiple times
  • Use hints strategically when you're stuck
  • Your best score (fewest moves) is saved for each difficulty
