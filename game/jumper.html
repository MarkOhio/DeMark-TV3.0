How to Play Jumping Cube

Desktop Controls:

  • Press SPACEBAR to jump
  • Hold SPACEBAR longer for higher jumps
  • Avoid obstacles on platforms
  • Collect yellow circles for bonus points
  • The game gets faster as you progress
  • Press P to pause or click Pause button

Mobile Controls:

  • Tap anywhere to jump
  • Longer taps = higher jumps
  • Avoid obstacles on platforms
  • Collect yellow circles for bonus points
  • The game gets faster as you progress
  • Tap Pause button to pause

Tips:

  • Time your jumps carefully for longer platforms
  • Plan your landing to avoid obstacles
  • Your best score is saved automatically
  • Pause when you need a break
