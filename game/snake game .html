How to Play Snake

Desktop Controls:

  • Use Arrow Keys (↑, ↓, ←, →) to change the snake's direction
  • Eat the red food to grow longer (+1 point)
  • Eat the rare blue food for bonus points (+5 points)
  • The game gets faster as you score more points
  • Avoid hitting the walls or your own tail

Mobile Controls:

  • Swipe in any direction to change the snake's direction
  • Or use the on-screen buttons
  • Eat the red food to grow longer (+1 point)
  • Eat the rare blue food for bonus points (+5 points)
  • The game gets faster as you score more points
  • Avoid hitting the walls or your own tail

Tips:

  • Plan your path to avoid getting trapped
  • The blue food appears randomly - grab it when you can!
  • Your best score is saved automatically
